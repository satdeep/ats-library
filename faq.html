Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Q1: What is the ATS Library System?
A: It's a web-based library management system for ATS residents to browse available books, borrow/return books, track reading status, and add new books to the collection.
Q2: How do I access the system?
A: Visit: https://ats-library-3d2aa.web.app

Account & Authentication

Q3: How do I sign up?
1. Click "Sign Up" on the homepage
2. Fill in: Name, Age, Email, Password, Apartment Number
3. Click "Sign Up"
Q4: I forgot my password. How to reset?
A: Password reset is disabled in this version. Contact the admin for assistance.
Q5: Why can't I see the "Add Book" button?
A: The button appears only after login. Ensure you're logged in with the correct email.

Borrowing & Returning

Q9: How do I borrow a book?
1. Go to "Available Books" tab
2. Click "Request" on your desired book
3. Owner will approve and set a meeting time/place
Q10: How do I return a book?
1. Go to "Borrowed Books" tab
2. Click "Return Book"
3. Set a return meeting time/place
4. Owner must confirm the return
Q11: What's the difference between statuses?
Available: Can be borrowed
Reading: Owner is reading it (not borrowable)
Requested: Someone asked to borrow it
Borrowed: Currently checked out
Pending Return: Return initiated but owner has yet to confirm that they have received the book back
